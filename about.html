Who We Are

AZ Legal Assistant (LawFlow) is a digital legal platform designed to bridge the gap between clients, legal professionals, and law students. Our aim is to make legal services, legal knowledge, and legal resources accessible, reliable, and affordable.

Our Vision

To become a trusted legal-tech platform in Pakistan that empowers individuals, lawyers, advocates, and law students through technology, knowledge-sharing, and professional collaboration.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality legal drafting, legal research, and professional legal hiring services, while also offering free access to academic, judicial, and sociological resources for learning and growth.
